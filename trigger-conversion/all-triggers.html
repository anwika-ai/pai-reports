Completeness & trust
  • Covers every distinct entryPointTask × entryPointElement that fires a payment_trigger_* event. Active = had events in the last 14 days; dormant = none recently (kept for completeness, dates only).
  • Share-dialog PRO toggles are under advanced_settings: Remove PAI branding (Remove logo), Password protect (Password protect), Alert when viewed (Alert on view), Set expiry (Expiry date).
  • Export formats share one trigger. Export-dialog PowerPoint, Google Slides and PDF all fire the single export_ppt · ExportPDF trigger (no per-format field) — not separable. Only Images (export_image) is distinct.
  • Tier (PRO vs GOLD) is not on the event — it comes from the product UI. ✓ badges are confirmed from screenshots; the rest are inferred.
  • (no_source) = popups with an empty source field (~18% of events) — a tracking gap, not a button. Thin samples (greyed, paid < 3) aren't rankable; buyers can see multiple triggers so paid counts overlap.
